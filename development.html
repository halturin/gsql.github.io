If you find GSQL prospective and are willing to contribute, you are welcome!

The next steps are:
  1. Download the source code from SVN repository and try to build it. How to do it is described in Download section.
  2. Subscribe to our mailing group. Also you can either join the IRC channel #GSQL where you can discuss with developers and contributors. Do not hesitate to ask for and advice. Our community is very friendly.
  3. Decide how you want to contribute. There are different directions:
    • software development (platform, engines, plugins)
    • documentation
    • graphics
    • translation
    • testing
    • packaging and distribution
Commit access to SVN repository you would gain once your become an active developer. Up to then you are free to send your modifications to any of project core developers.
